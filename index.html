BCNNM stands for Biological Cellular Neural Network Modelling and its goal is to provide a simulation of a brain development in living organisms. We are starting from single cell and a set of biological rules that describing behaviour of the cell-cell interactions. During the simulation, network evolves (new neural cells are formed, some of them are dying, others are forming synaptic connections). After the growth phase there is a learning process somewhat similar to supervised learning in artificial neural networks (ANN).
